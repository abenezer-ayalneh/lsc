Terms of use

These ground rules apply to everyone using Firhill Road Sports Ground. Non-compliance may result in being asked to leave or barred from the site.

Safety & security

  • Parking is at the owner's risk.
  • Drive slowly — children are present.
  • The Consortium accepts no liability for personal property.

Changing & amenities

  • Use the changing rooms to change, not the field.
  • Use the toilets provided.
  • Clean up after yourselves and use the bins.
  • Do not clean boots in the showers.
  • Use the warm-up area, not the pitches, to warm up.

Conduct

  • No dogs.
  • No swearing.
  • No smoking.
  • No alcohol.
