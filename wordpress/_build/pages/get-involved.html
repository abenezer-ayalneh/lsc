Making a Difference — our youth programme

Our “Making a Difference” programme supports young people with GCSE and SAT preparation classes (English, Maths and Science), social and cultural education workshops, one-to-one mentorship, and encouragement into enterprise — delivered in partnership with organisations including The Prince's Trust.

Hire the ground
